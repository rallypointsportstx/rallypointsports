Rally Point Sports is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization in Forney, TX dedicated to building character, leadership, and athletic skill by removing financial barriers to competition.

Our Mission

We believe every child deserves the chance to compete, grow, and thrive. Rally Point Sports was founded to empower youth through accessible sports programs that focus on more than just the game.


We are committed to:

  • Removing financial barriers for families.
  • Developing leadership and character on and off the field.
  • Creating a supportive community for Forney youth.

Our Programs

Competitive Soccer

High-level coaching and league play for youth of all ages. We focus on technical skills, tactical understanding, and teamwork.

Team Sports Funding

Financial assistance for equipment, league fees, and tournament costs to ensure no player is left on the sidelines.

Leadership Development

Mentorship programs designed to help young athletes become leaders in their schools and communities.
